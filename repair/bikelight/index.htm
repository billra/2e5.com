Bike Light Repair

tags: fix
2e5 » repair » bikelight

This bicycle light has a broken mounting bracket. I'm too cheap to buy a new one and I think the mounting system can be improved.

The drill bit size is 3mm. Solid copper wire is wonderful repair material.

Two holes are drilled through the old mounting bracket receptacle.

Wires are cut and inserted into the holes.

Bike light is now ready for mounting on bicycle!
